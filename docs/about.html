Our mission — To provide impactful growth solutions

This isn’t just a statement; it’s our commitment to every client we partner with. We dig into the specifics of your business, explore your potential, and build revenue programs that translate bold goals into predictable results.

Our vision — A world where business growth is no longer a challenge

Imagine a world where growing your business is as smooth as sailing on a calm sea. That’s the future we’re building—a reality where every revenue leader has a reliable partner, every team has the tools to excel, and every growth target is within reach.

Belkins’ values

Our history

  1. 2017

    Belkins is founded to reinvent the way B2B companies generate qualified meetings.

  2. 2018

    The team scales globally, partnering with ambitious brands across North America and Europe.

  3. 2019

    Belkins earns industry recognition for outbound expertise and client satisfaction.

  4. 2020

    Folderly launches, transforming email deliverability for revenue teams worldwide.

  5. 2021

    We open new hubs and expand our leadership bench to accelerate customer success.

  6. 2022

    Charge joins the portfolio, empowering SDRs with a purpose-built outreach platform.

  7. 2023

    Belkins is honored with global awards for innovation, culture, and client outcomes.

  8. 2024

    We continue to diversify our products and services to keep pace with modern buyers.
