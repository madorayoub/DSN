3 Transition
2 min
Script "So if I understand: You're at [situation], you want to get to [goal], but [gap] is stopping you. Right?"
(Get confirmation BEFORE pitching)

"Based on what you shared... here's specifically how we'd solve that for you..."
4 Customized Pitch
7 min
  1. Link Features to Pain: Don't just list features. Say "Because you have [problem], we do [feature]."
  2. Use Their Language: Repeat the words they used in discovery.
  3. Do the ROI Math: Calculate with them.
Example "You said you're tired of chasing bad leads. What if we brought you 10-15 pre-qualified commercial leads ensuring you only talk to decision makers?"
⚠️ STOP SAYING
"We have a three-pronged approach..." (Generic pitches kill deals)
5 Committing (Temp Check)
3 min

Run this BEFORE handling objections or closing.

Step 1: Temperature Check
"On a scale of 1-10, where 10 is 'let's do this right now' and 1 is 'this isn't for me', where are you?"
Step 2: Next Steps
"Here's what happens after you say yes: Onboarding call Tuesday, intake form, leads start in 7 days."
Step 3: Investment
"Total monthly investment is $3,500 ($2k management + $1.5k ad spend). Does that work for your budget?"
Step 4: Logistics
"Residential leads start in 7 days. Commercial leads take 2-3 weeks to ramp up properly."
6 Assumptive Close
2 min
The Close Sequence 1. "This seems like a perfect fit for getting you from [$X] to [$Y]. Agree?"
2. "Let's get you started. When works for onboarding - [option A] or [option B]?"
3. Book the time.
4. "Great. Do you have a Visa or Mastercard?"
5. Get card details.
6. "Perfect. You'll get the agreement shortly. See you [day/time]."
⚠️ DO NOT GIVE THEM AGREEMENT TO 'REVIEW'
This is how you lose them!
7 Handle Objections
5 min

Use the L.A.E.R. Framework (Listen, Acknowledge, Explore, Respond).

OBJECTION DON'T SAY ✗ DO SAY ✓
"Need to think" "How much time?" "Is it the investment, timing, or you're not sure we can deliver?"
"Talk to partner" "When can we follow up?" "What will they want to know? Let me answer those NOW."
"Been burned before" "We're different" "Tell me what happened. Here's how we prevent that..."
"See agreement?" "I'll send it" "Let me make sure this is a fit first..." [more discovery]
