Careers

Direct Sales Network (DSN) is a fully distributed revenue organization where strategists, SDRs, marketers, and data scientists collaborate to build predictable growth engines for complex B2B companies. We hire curious people who love experimentation, thrive on cross-functional collaboration, and measure success by the meetings and revenue we create for clients.

Grow with a distributed revenue team

DSN operates across North America, Europe, and LATAM, so you can work where you have the strongest impact. Every teammate pairs with a mentor, follows a personalized enablement track, and receives quarterly learning stipends to deepen channel expertise. From omnichannel cadences to RevOps automation, you will work with playbooks that set the standard for how modern pipeline teams operate.

We prioritize outcomes over office hours. Flexible schedules, asynchronous collaboration, and clear goals let you manage your energy while delivering measurable pipeline. Benefits include competitive compensation, wellness days, and home-office stipends that make remote work sustainable.

Roles we hire throughout the year

  • Sales Development Representatives: Run multichannel prospecting, personalize outreach, and book executive-level meetings.
  • Engagement Managers: Partner with clients to design revenue programs, analyze data, and keep execution aligned to KPIs.
  • RevOps Specialists: Architect CRM, intent, and analytics workflows that give teams a unified view of funnel performance.
  • Content & Paid Strategists: Build messaging, enablement assets, and paid campaigns that amplify outbound momentum.

Don’t see your ideal role listed? We regularly open contractor-to-hire engagements and internships across analytics, research, and creative teams. Reach out at contact@directsales.network with a short note about how you can help revenue leaders grow faster.

How our hiring process works

  1. Application review: We evaluate your experience, metrics, and motivation to align you with the right pod and vertical.
  2. Skills conversation: Meet a DSN leader to walk through scenarios, past wins, and how you approach prioritization.
  3. Paid simulation: Collaborate on a mini-project using our frameworks so you can experience our workflow before joining.
  4. Offer & onboarding: If it’s a fit, we map your 30-60-90 day plan, assign a mentor, and set your first revenue goals.

We believe diverse perspectives strengthen our client work. DSN is an equal opportunity employer and welcomes teammates from every background. If you require accommodations during the hiring process, email us and we’ll respond within two business days.
