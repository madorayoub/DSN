Our Process

How Hiring Works

A transparent, candidate-focused process designed to find the right mutual fit.

1

Application Review

We evaluate your experience, metrics, and motivation. We'll reach out within 5 business days if there's a potential fit.

2

Skills Conversation

Meet a DSN leader to discuss scenarios, past wins, and how you approach prioritization. This is a two-way conversation.

3

Paid Simulation

Collaborate on a mini-project using our frameworks. Experience our workflow and see if the role fits your working style.

4

Offer & Onboarding

If it's a fit, we map your 30-60-90 day plan, assign a mentor, and set your first revenue goals. Welcome aboard!

Accommodations: We welcome teammates from every background. If you require accommodations during the hiring process, email us at contact@directsales.network and we'll respond within two business days.
